Trouble Maintaining Your Plants?

Watch this video to help you autonomously water and take care of your plant! It provides an environmentally friendly way for saving water and energy while helping your plants grow healthy. Never let one of your plants die again!

I will be trying to make my own variation of this automatic plant watering system but with the same idea in mind. I hope to introduce a sustainable way for helping the environment by conserving water and energy usage.
